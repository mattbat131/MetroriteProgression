MeteoriteProgression

Determines the affect of meteorite collisions on Earth

What We Do

Four high school rising seniors got together as Team ApricotBroccoliCarrotDurian for the Space Apps Next Generation Hackathon to create MeteoriteProgression. It showcases the impact over time on vegetation, rainfall, temperature, and etc. after a meteorite falls onto Earth from 2000 to present. We got our data from NASA.
